# Legal Review Checklist

PactRide is a public-interest protocol project licensed under Apache-2.0. The repository license is now standard and simple, but operating a real transportation service or creating official certification, payment, insurance, or safety programs would still require qualified legal review.

## Repository and contribution questions

- Is Apache-2.0 correctly applied to all current repository material?
- Are third-party notices and dependencies recorded where required?
- Should the project add a `NOTICE` file when it has attribution notices that need preservation?
- Should contributors use Developer Certificate of Origin sign-off?
- When is written corporate contribution authorization necessary?
- How should AI-assisted contributions be disclosed and reviewed for provenance?
- Are patent ownership and contributor patent grants understood by maintainers?

## Trademark and public representation

- Who owns or administers the PactRide name and future visual marks?
- Should the mark be registered, and in which jurisdictions or classes?
- Which descriptive uses remain automatically permitted?
- How should official, compatible, certified, and endorsed claims be distinguished?
- How can a certification program avoid deceptive or anticompetitive conduct?

## Operational and commercial questions

Before a real pilot or service, review:

- local transportation licensing and dispatch rules;
- driver and vehicle requirements;
- insurance obligations;
- accessibility and nondiscrimination requirements;
- privacy, retention, breach-notification, and location-data rules;
- payment processing, refunds, taxes, and money-transmission boundaries;
- incident response and emergency disclaimers;
- warranties, indemnities, limitations of liability, and support commitments;
- entity, accounting, grant, sponsorship, and tax structure.

## Documents to review

- `LICENSE`
- `LICENSING.md`
- `TRADEMARK.md`
- `CONTRIBUTOR_POLICY.md`
- `CONTRIBUTING.md`
- `GOVERNANCE.md`
- `PRIVACY.md`
- `THREAT_MODEL.md`
- any future pilot agreement, certification rules, privacy policy, terms of service, or commercial contract

## Current safeguard

PactRide remains a pre-implementation research RFC. The Apache-2.0 license permits development and commercial use, but it does not certify legal compliance, safety, insurance, identity, payment protection, or readiness for public ride operations.

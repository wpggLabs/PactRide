# Independent Review Request

PactRide is a pre-implementation research RFC. This repository seeks falsifiable, evidence-backed review; it does not seek endorsements or declarations of production readiness.

## Useful review scopes

- ride lifecycle, event semantics, schemas, and test vectors;
- privacy and location-metadata leakage;
- cryptography, identity, relay, and transport assumptions;
- accessibility, abuse, safety, and responsibility boundaries;
- interoperability, conformance, and operational limitations;
- licensing, contributor provenance, and trademark-policy clarity.

## Review submission

Open a GitHub issue or pull request that identifies the exact repository commit, review scope, evidence or reproduction steps, severity, conflicts of interest, and recommended change. Maintainers record accepted reviews and dispositions in `REVIEW_LOG.md`.

Reviews must not include real trip histories, personal data, credentials, confidential material, or unsupported claims of certification, safety, or legal approval.
